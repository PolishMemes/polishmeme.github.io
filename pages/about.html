Introduction

A bot that will draw you into Polish memes.

About this Bot

The bot guarantees 100% security. It does not store any detailed data about clients or their servers. It operates 24/7 and is fully SFW.

Technology

The bot was designed in JavaScript, but was later rewritten in Python and now operates in Discord.py.

The bot is hosted on daki.cc hosting.

Getting Started

The bot is simple to use. Just invite it to your server and type /meme!
